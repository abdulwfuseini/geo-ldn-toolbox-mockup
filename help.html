Help - LDN Toolbox

The LDN Toolbox is a platform designed to assist users in managing land degradation neutrality. Here are some tips to help you navigate the site:

  • Home: Learn about the purpose of the LDN Toolbox and its functionality.
  • Toolbox: Explore a collection of tools, datasets, and use cases. Use the filters to narrow down your search based on scale, ILUP phase, tool type, and more.
  • Filters: Use the filters on the left sidebar of the Toolbox page to customize the tools displayed based on your specific needs.

If you have any questions, please contact us at support@ldntoolbox.org.
